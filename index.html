Lab data visualizations

Interactive explorers for Tasic 2018 mouse V1 GABAergic interneurons. Each app fits a K=4 archetype NMF (consensus over 20 inits) on a per-subclass HVG panel, then projects ~3,000 broader-transcriptome genes onto the same archetypes via NNLS. Two rotatable 3D tetrahedrons: cells (W barycentric) on the left, genes (H barycentric) on the right. Hover to cross-recolor.

Source: Tasic et al. 2018, mouse VISp (anterior lateral motor + primary visual). Panel HVG selected per subclass on log-CPM via Seurat-v3 variance. Outlier subtypes (long-projecting Sst Chodl, chandelier Pvalb Vipr2, etc.) were dropped before NMF. Hovering shows whether a gene is a panel HVG or NNLS-imputed from the broader transcriptome — imputed loadings for low-expression genes (most GPCRs) are noisy and should be read as rough estimates.
Built with Plotly · self-contained HTML, no server.
